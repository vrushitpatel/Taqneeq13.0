About Us

Taqneeq, MPSTME’s Annual Technical Fest is one of the biggest fests witnessed by Mumbai. Taqneeq is a three-day celebration of technology with a rich history which witnesses the presence of the who’s who of the nation. Taqneeq is fast becoming the paradigm of how a technical fest should be and it is technology in its purest form. It is a technological marvel which provides dreamers and believers with a stage to showcase their talents and how one can conquer almost anything with the right technology.

Team Taqneeq strives to achieve the pinnacle of quality and bring out the finest in everyone and it is now back with it’s 12th edition and is all set to get bigger and better this year with Taqneeq all set to be expanded into a three day fest to be held on 27th, 28th of February and 1st of March.

Theme

The 21st century race to send humans into space consists of buying a piece of land on the Moon, creating potential plans for 15 minute space vacations, and an extensive search for habitable planets. Now, instead of nuclear superpowers warring to emerge victorious within the Earth's orbit, companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin and Virgin Galactic are battling to make space travel and colonization more readily available and affordable.

From our earliest hunter gatherer steps, we have endeavored to explore and learn more about the far regions of the planet we inhabit. This instinct has allowed us to build civilization and expand it into the billions that walk the Earth today. Exploring into space is just an extension of our primal instinct and an opportunity to not only discover new worlds, but also to build advanced technologies. Someday, humans would like to traverse the far ends of the infinitely large universe due to the awe they possess for the enigma of space.
